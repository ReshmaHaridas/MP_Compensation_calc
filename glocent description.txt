The Sales Compensation System is a web based solution which perform the compensation calculation based on certain rules and provides reports. 
This will be used by  Finance department of an Organization to decide how much Commission should be paid to their employees for the individual sales done.
The portal handles three type of users:- Commission Admin,Manager and Employee.
Main modules include:-
   	- User configuration
   	- Rule Configuration
   	- Managing Sales data
    	- Compensation Calculation
    	- Reports
